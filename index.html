JCU Professional Partners: Pre-Health

Project Description

"JCU Professional Partners: Pre-Health" is a web application for John Carroll University Pre-Health students looking to expland their network and connect with alumni and practitioners in the health field. Students who have created an account are able to view registered practitioners, search and filter through that list, and view practitioners’ profiles in order to connect with them.

I created this website with a team of four other students during the fall 2018 semester for our Computer Science Capstone final project. My main responsibility was creating the front-end of the project.

This web application will soon be launched, but is currently available via GitHub.
